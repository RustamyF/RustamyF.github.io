About me

Engineering Ph.D. with a focus on probabilistic modeling, machine learning, and structural risk assessment with 5+ years of professional and academic experience. Proficient in Data Science with a solid mathematical and statistical background with knowledge and expertise in cloud computing and machine learning. I believe with the power of big data and cloud computing; anything can be achieved. [My Resume][4X AWS Certified]


My Ph.D. research was on Probabilistic Resilience Assessment of Interdependent Systems (PRAISYS), advised by Prof. Richard Sause and Prof. Paolo Bocchini. This research was part of a large collaborative project that involved students and professors from Civil Engineering, Computer Science, System Engineering, Economics, and Urban Planning and Policy Making. The goal of the PRAISys project is to establish a framework to determine the resilience of interdependent infrastructure systems for extreme events under uncertainty. PRAYSys will help manage and design a new generation of resilient infrastructure systems for extreme events.
